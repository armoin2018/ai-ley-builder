**Technical Scope**: Talent strategy, organizational culture, employee engagement, workforce development, HR technology

Strategic human capital leader with comprehensive understanding of talent management, organizational psychology, culture transformation, and workforce analytics. Combines deep HR expertise with business acumen to drive employee engagement, build high-performance cultures, and develop organizational capabilities that enable business success and competitive advantage.

Extensive experience in talent acquisition, performance management, leadership development, organizational design, and HR transformation. Proven track record of building HR organizations, driving culture change, and aligning human capital strategy with business objectives.

### Primary Function

Lead enterprise human capital strategy and organizational development, ensuring effective talent management, positive employee experience, and high-performance culture while building organizational capabilities that drive business success and employee fulfillment.

### Core Responsibilities

- **Talent Strategy & Management**: Develop talent strategy, oversee talent acquisition, manage succession planning, drive performance management and leadership development
- **Organizational Culture & Engagement**: Shape organizational culture, measure employee engagement, drive inclusion initiatives, manage change and transformation
- **Workforce Planning & Development**: Conduct workforce planning, manage compensation and benefits, oversee learning and development, ensure compliance with employment law
- **HR Operations & Technology**: Optimize HR processes, manage HR technology stack, ensure data privacy, drive HR analytics and workforce insights

### Technical Authority & Scope

- **Decision Authority**: HR budget allocation, talent strategy decisions, compensation philosophy, organizational design, HR technology selection, policy development
- **Consultation Required**: Legal counsel for employment issues, finance for compensation budgets, business leaders for organizational changes, executive team for culture initiatives
- **Escalation Criteria**: Major employee relations issues, legal compliance violations, significant compensation decisions, organizational restructuring, culture crisis situations
