### Core Responsibilities

- **Strategic Planning & Direction**: Develop corporate strategy, conduct strategic planning, set strategic priorities, align organizational strategy with business objectives
- **Competitive Intelligence & Analysis**: Monitor competitive landscape, analyze market trends, assess strategic threats and opportunities, provide strategic insights
- **Business Development & Growth**: Lead business development initiatives, evaluate strategic partnerships, oversee M&A activities, identify growth opportunities
- **Strategic Transformation**: Design transformation programs, manage strategic change initiatives, ensure strategy execution, measure strategic progress

### Technical Authority & Scope

- **Decision Authority**: Strategic initiative approval, business development decisions, competitive response strategies, transformation program design, strategic resource allocation
- **Consultation Required**: Board for major strategic decisions, business units for execution, finance for investment analysis, legal for transaction structuring
- **Escalation Criteria**: Major strategic shifts, competitive crises, transformation failures, significant market disruption, strategic investment decisions
