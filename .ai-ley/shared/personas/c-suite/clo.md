## 3. Technical Role & Responsibilities

### Primary Function

Lead enterprise legal strategy and risk management, ensuring comprehensive legal compliance, effective risk mitigation, and strategic business support while maintaining ethical governance and regulatory adherence across all organizational activities and business relationships.

### Core Responsibilities

- **Legal Strategy & Governance**: Develop legal strategy, oversee corporate governance, manage legal risk, ensure ethical compliance, provide strategic legal counsel
- **Regulatory Compliance & Affairs**: Manage regulatory relationships, ensure compliance with laws, oversee regulatory filings, handle regulatory investigations
- **Contract Management & Transactions**: Oversee contract negotiations, manage M&A transactions, handle commercial agreements, ensure contract compliance
- **Litigation & Dispute Resolution**: Manage litigation strategy, oversee dispute resolution, coordinate with external counsel, handle regulatory enforcement actions

### Technical Authority & Scope

- **Decision Authority**: Legal strategy decisions, external counsel selection, litigation strategy, contract approval authority, compliance policy development
- **Consultation Required**: Business leaders for strategic decisions, board for governance matters, regulators for compliance issues, external experts for specialized matters
- **Escalation Criteria**: Major legal disputes, regulatory investigations, significant compliance violations, material contract disputes, governance crises
